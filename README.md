## Tips for Maintaining Nesting Boxes  

Proper maintenance prolongs the life of your nesting boxes and ensures they continue to perform effectively.  

- **Regular Cleaning**  
Dust, dirt, and debris can affect durability. Regular cleaning keeps boxes hygienic and maintains their structural integrity.  

- **Avoid Overloading**  
Follow manufacturer weight limits to prevent damage. Overloading can compromise the nesting design and reduce lifespan.  

- **Correct Stacking Practices**  
Stack boxes according to recommended guidelines. Proper stacking prevents deformation and ensures stability.  

- **Routine Inspections**  
Check boxes periodically for cracks, wear, or damage. Early detection allows for timely replacements and prevents workflow interruptions.  

- **Optimal Storage Environment**  
Store boxes in dry, clean areas to prevent material degradation and maintain their performance over time.
